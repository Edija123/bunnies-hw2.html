About Bunnies

Bunnies, or rabbits, are small mammals known for their long ears, fluffy tails, and gentle personalities. They belong to the family Leporidae.

They live in a variety of environments worldwide and are often kept as beloved pets due to their friendly nature.

Across cultures, bunnies symbolize fertility, springtime, and playfulness. They are also popular figures in folklore and stories.

Fun Facts

  • Bunnies can rotate their ears almost 180 degrees.
  • Their teeth never stop growing.
  • Like cats, happy rabbits purr when they're content and relaxed.

Most common bunny colors

  1. White
  2. Black
  3. Brown
  4. Gray
  5. Spotted
