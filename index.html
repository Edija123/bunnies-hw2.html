Bunnies, or rabbits, are small mammals known for their long ears, fluffy tails, and gentle personalities. They belong to the family Leporidae. They live in a variety of environments worldwide and are often kept as beloved pets due to their friendly nature. Across cultures, bunnies symbolize fertility, springtime, and playfulness. They are also popular figures in folklore and stories.
