Welcome to the Bunny World!

Bunnies are adorable mammals known for their long ears, fluffy tails, and gentle personalities. They are loved worldwide as pets and appear in folklore, stories, and art.

About Bunnies

Bunnies live in a variety of environments worldwide and are often kept as beloved pets due to their friendly nature. Across cultures, they symbolize fertility, springtime, and playfulness.

Fun Facts

  • Bunnies can rotate their ears almost 180 degrees.
  • Their teeth never stop growing.
  • Like cats, happy rabbits purr when content.

Most common bunny colors

  1. White
  2. Black
  3. Brown
  4. Gray
  5. Spotted
